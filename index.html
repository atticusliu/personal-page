Projects

LightRunner
An Android game in which you play as a man in space who fiercely fights off an endless onslaught of enemies by refracting light from a source back toward the enemy. Built with StudentRND!

MovieHoppr
Ever wanted to see what movies you can catch in one visit to the theater, or just a simple way to see the movies in nearby theaters? With MovieHoppr and the Gracenote API, you'll be able to see exactly that and without the clutter of other apps. Built at Cal Hacks 2014!
